# Contributing to ERP Project

Thank you for considering contributing to our ERP project! We appreciate your help in improving the project. Here are some guidelines to follow when contributing:

## How to Contribute

1. **Fork the Repository**: Create your own fork of the repository.
2. **Create a Branch**: Create a new branch for your feature or bug fix. Use descriptive names.
3. **Make Changes**: Implement your changes in your branch.
4. **Test Your Changes**: Ensure that your changes work as expected and pass any tests.
5. **Commit Your Changes**: Write a clear commit message that explains your changes.
6. **Push to Your Fork**: Push your changes to your forked repository.
7. **Create a Pull Request**: Open a pull request to the main repository's `main` branch, describing your changes and why they should be merged.

## Code of Conduct

Please adhere to our code of conduct in all your interactions. Be respectful and considerate.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a feature request, please create an issue in the issue tracker.

## Questions

If you have any questions or need help, feel free to reach out to us in the community channels.

Happy contributing!
